Mythological Creatures

Short video For Kids

Join us in exploring some of the most famous creatures and notorious beasts in Greek Mythology. Learn about the Minotaur (half man and half bull), centaurs (half horse and half man), Pegasus (flying horse), Chimera (combination of a lion, snake, and goat), Cerberus (three headed dog who guards the underworld), Cyclopes (one eyed giants), Hydra (monster with regenerating snake heads), Medusa (monster with snakes for hair) , griffins (half eagle and half lion), satyrs (half man and half goat), and nymphs. Be introduced to the most famous of these characters in Greek myths such as Chiron and Polyphemus and hear about some of the exciting heroes in ancient Greek Mythology such as Perseus, Odysseus, and Bellerophon. The video is set up in format where students or children can guess the beast or creature that is hidden. After three clues, the students or children can see the answer and learn a little more about the famous characters of ancient Greek Mythology. Great for teachers who want to introduce kids to some of the Greek myths, creatures, and beasts. This video provides a format for students and kids to learn and demonstrate that they know some of these famous and notorious characters from long ago.
